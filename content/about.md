## Hello World!

I’m Francesco, you might know me online as **Airscript**.

I’ve always been curious about the world around me. *Videogames, sports, cinematography*, and yes, *dinosaurs*. Especially dinosaurs.

Out of all my interests, **computer science** is the one that truly stuck. The idea that I could build things, *tools that actually improve people’s lives*, hooked me early. What started as curiosity turned into **passion**, and eventually into my career.

Today, I work as a **software engineer**, currently serving as **Head of Technology at Beelzebub**, where we build *AI-powered cybersecurity systems* to make the internet a safer place. From *autonomous SOC tools* to *full-stack AI honeypots*, we focus on detecting and neutralizing threats across modern infrastructures.

Alongside that, I work as a **freelance engineer**, helping bring ideas to life through code, whether that’s *CLI tools*, *web applications*, or *scalable systems*. I also support others through *interview prep*, *resume reviews*, and *technical guidance*.

I’m deeply involved in **open source**, where I enjoy building projects that are both *useful* and *fun*, and sharing them freely. I also contribute to tech communities and volunteer with initiatives like **Plastic Free**, promoting collaboration and positive impact beyond code.

If you’re reaching out for freelance work, advice, or just to connect, feel free to email me and include the word `dinosaur` so I know you made it this far.
